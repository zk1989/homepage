Ceramics

Some time ago I took a one-year class in ceramics. I mostly enjoyed creating tiles - inventing different ways of arranging the same shape. My friend then made this nice photo of some of them.

Earl grey tea

I like the way it smells, looks and tastes. IMO it's way better than any coffee. There are many variations and brands to choose from, but sometimes it's hard for me to break out of my routinely chosen one.

Biking

One of my favorite things about riding a bike is watching its light on the road in front of me when it's dark. It's an old bike, and it has dynamo lights - so they only light up when the wheels are spinning. This gives a nice, flickering effect.
